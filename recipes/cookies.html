Chocolate Chip Cookie



Milks best friend is the chocolate chip cookie. Although there are thousands of ways to make them, I will be showing you the absolute best.


Ingredients

  • Butter 280g
  • Flour 400g
  • Salt 10g
  • Eggs 2
  • Brown Sugar 450g
  • Chocolate Chip 100g

Directions for Best Cookies Ever

  1. Begin by mixing the butter with the sugar. Mix until you achieve a thick creamy texture
  2. Add one egg and mix completly, repeat with second egg
  3. Mix salt and flour together is seperate bowl
  4. Add flour and salt to butter mix and mix thoroughly
  5. Mix in the chocolate chips and roll dough into a cylinder
  6. place dough in refridgerator untill firm
  7. Cut dough into small cylinders and bake at 350f for 12 minutes
