Mexican Rice Recipe


Rice is a staple dish in cuisines all over the world, and while it is prepared in various different ways, Mexican Rice stands out among the rest because of its rich flavor.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Rice
  • 2 cups of Water
  • 1/2 cup of tomoato puree
  • 1 tbsp of chicken broth seasoning

Instructions

  1. Begin by warming up the water in a pot.
  2. while waiting for water to boil, wash rice thuroughly
  3. Once water begins to boil turn heat down to medium low
  4. Add seasoning and puree to water, stir untill mixed properly
  5. Add Rice and cover pot, leave on medium low for 18 minutes
  6. Enjoy
