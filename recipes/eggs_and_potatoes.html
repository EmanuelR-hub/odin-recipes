Eggs and Potatoes



Eggs and Potatoes is one of the most common breakfast dishes that has been made a million different ways. I will be showing you my person favorite method, and I am sure it will become your favorite as well.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Potatoe
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • A Pinch of Pepper
  • 1tbsp of butter
  • 1 tbsp of oil(I personally use olive oil)

Instruction

  1. Begin by heating up a frying pan with the tablespoon of oil
  2. While waitng for oil to heat up, begin cutting potatoe into small squares (I recommend leaving the skin oon the potatoe)
  3. Once oil is hot, add the table spoon of butter and add potatoes as soon as butter turn a golden color
  4. Spread Potatoes evenly among pan and leave for 5 minutes on medium heat
  5. Crack eggs into a bowl a mix thoroughly until egg is an even color
  6. Add Salt and Pepper to Eggs and mix thoroughly
  7. after 5 minutes move potatoes around and let set for a few more minutes
  8. Repeat until potatoes achieve uniform golden color
  9. Add eggs to pan and let sit for 30s/1min, mix until eggs is cooked
